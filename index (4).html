Our Story

Born in the mountains of Ladakh.

The Learning Pass started with a simple belief: where you are born should never determine how far you can go. Founded by students from New Delhi, we saw firsthand the gap between what Ladakhi children deserved and what they had access to.

In collaboration with the National Book Trust of India and local school partners, we began delivering curated books directly into students' hands. Since then, we've grown into a full educational platform, running live online classes, AI literacy workshops, and structured academic support programs.

We are not a one-time charity. We build recurring, scalable systems that give children ongoing support across academic years.

What We Do

Four pillars of access.

📚

Book Distribution

Working with the National Book Trust and local partners, we deliver curated books to students who would otherwise have no access, over 2,000 books placed in children's hands across Ladakh.

🖥️

Online Classes

Our volunteers teach live online sessions in English, Mathematics, and Science, connecting students in remote Ladakhi villages with quality instruction from across India.

🤝

School Seminars

We visit partner schools for structured in-person seminars, mentoring sessions, and interactive workshops, building direct relationships with students and faculty on the ground.

💡

Digital Literacy

From AI fundamentals to internet safety, we equip students in remote Ladakh with the digital skills needed to navigate and thrive in a rapidly changing world.
